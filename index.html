Current Issues

Vietnam faces a massive amount of daily household waste, with plastic waste making up a significant percentage but rarely getting recycled.

Lack of source separation, with many residents not having the habit of sorting waste, makes processing difficult and increases environmental burden.

Some people still lack awareness in maintaining public cleanliness by littering or burning trash, causing air pollution.
