Become an Ambassador of the Project

If you value and appreciate life, and want to contribute your efforts to serve and develop the community, join us to become an Ambassador of Green Environment
You can join through many ways:

Let's plant hope, plant life, and spread green to every corner of the country.

01 Join the WasteLess project in your local area

Become a pioneer in organizing waste reduction, recycling, and green living activities in your area. Together, we can build a WasteLess model closely linked to the local community.

02 Connect with government agencies

Collaborate with government agencies to develop a waste management plan, implement recycling collection points, and launch campaigns to raise awareness of environmental protection.

03 Connect with businesses

Find partners in funding environmentally friendly products, support recycling activities, or implement the "office without waste" model.

04 Connect with local communities

Collaborate with local communities to organize waste sorting programs at the source, exchange reusable items, or convert waste into useful resources.

05 Connect and communicate

Find and connect with businesses to create a sustainable funding source for tree planting and forest projects.
